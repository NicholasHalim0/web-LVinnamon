History

LVinnamon, famous for its irresistible cinnamon rolls, was established in 1972 by Luigi Vilton. With a passion for baking and a dedication to using top-quality ingredients, Luigi perfected his cinnamon roll recipe, captivating the local community. LVinnamon's cinnamon rolls quickly gained popularity, and their reputation spread beyond the town of Hyrule. People from neighboring towns would make special trips to indulge in the heavenly swirls of cinnamon and tender dough. Luigi's son, Mario, joined the business in the 1980s, ushering in a period of expansion and innovation. LVinnamon introduced new flavors and variations, becoming a beloved worldwide institution. They embraced online presence in the 2023, shipping their delectable cinnamon rolls. Today, LVinnamon continues to delight customers with their flavorful cinnamon rolls, maintaining Luigi's legacy of excellence.
